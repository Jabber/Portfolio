Have any questions?

This FAQ section aims to provide answers to commonly asked questions about the artist's work, background, and services. Should you not find the information you are looking for, do not hesitate to reach out for further assistance.

What's your experience?

I've worked in Australia, Switzerland, and Budapest, lived in Italy and the UK and travelled 10% of the globe to help clients and broaden my perspective. I've worked as a product manager, data scientist, financial expert on trading and management consultant for many years across several industries: digital consulting, pharmaceuticals, energy, higher education, digital marketing, sales and HR, just to name a few.

Why data science?

I always had an inclination towards more analytical subjects; hence I've spent the better part of my education related to maths, finance, economics and econometrics. Data science came as a natural path where these skills could be combined.

What are you up to nowadays?

Revolutionising consulting! I've led and built teams for a long time and performed digital transformations, learning from each of those experiences. The culmination of these experiences will help us make the best product on the market, taking us towards realising a large-scale impact on the industry.
